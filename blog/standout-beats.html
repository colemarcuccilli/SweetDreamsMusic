Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. Over-Processing:

  • Signs:
    • Loss of impact
    • Muddy mix
    • Weak transients
  • Solutions:
    • Start with great samples
    • Process purposefully
    • Compare to references

2. Poor Sample Selection:

  • Issues:
    • Weak foundation
    • Lack of cohesion
    • Translation problems
  • Solutions:
    • Curate quality samples
    • Test on multiple systems
    • Layer thoughtfully

3. Overcrowded Arrangements:

  • Problems:
    • Lack of space
    • Unclear focus
    • Energy loss
  • Solutions:
    • Use space effectively
    • Remove unnecessary elements
    • Focus on core groove
