Processing Techniques

1. Kick Processing:

  • EQ Settings:
    • High-pass at 20Hz
    • Boost 60-100Hz
    • Cut 200-300Hz
  • Compression:
    • Fast attack (1-5ms)
    • Medium release (50-100ms)
    • 4:1 ratio typical

2. Snare Treatment:

  • EQ Approach:
    • Boost 200Hz for body
    • Enhance 3-5kHz for crack
    • High shelf for air
  • Effects:
    • Parallel compression
    • Reverb send
    • Saturation

Professional Insight:

Layer your drums thoughtfully. Two or three well-chosen layers often sound better than many competing sounds.
