Best Audio Interfaces for Recording in 2025

Reading time: 15 min Category: Equipment

Why You Need an Audio Interface

A high-quality audio interface is the most important piece of equipment in your recording studio. It converts your analog sound into digital audio that your computer can process.

Top 5 Audio Interfaces for 2025

1. Focusrite Scarlett 2i2 ($179)

Best for: Beginners and home studios

  • Crystal-clear preamps
  • Low latency monitoring
  • USB-C connection

2. Universal Audio Volt 276 ($299)

Best for: Professional home studios

  • Built-in analog compression
  • Vintage tube emulation
  • Professional sound quality
