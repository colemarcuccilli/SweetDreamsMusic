## RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, the Company operates an online beat store and music production marketplace located at sweetdreamsmusic.com (the "Platform"); and

**WHEREAS**, the Producer is a music producer who creates original musical compositions, instrumentals, and beats (collectively, "Beats"); and

**WHEREAS**, the Producer desires to list, market, and sell Beats through the Platform, and the Company desires to facilitate such sales under the terms and conditions set forth herein;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants, promises, and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. GRANT OF RIGHTS

**1.1 Non-Exclusive Listing Rights.** Producer hereby grants to Sweet Dreams Music a **non-exclusive** right and license to list, display, market, promote, stream previews of, and facilitate the sale of Producer's Beats through the Platform. This grant does not transfer ownership of any Beat to the Company.

**1.2 Scope of License.** The rights granted herein are limited to the operation of the Platform and the facilitation of sales between Producer and buyers ("Licensees" or "Buyers"). The Company shall not use, license, sublicense, or sell Producer's Beats for its own commercial purposes outside the scope of Platform operations without prior written consent from the Producer.

**1.3 Marketing and Promotion.** Producer grants the Company the right to use Beat titles, audio previews (including short clips or snippets), Producer's name/alias, and associated artwork for the purpose of marketing and promoting the Platform, including but not limited to social media posts, email campaigns, website features, and advertisements.

**1.4 Multiple Platforms.** Producer retains the right to list and sell their Beats on other platforms, websites, or through direct sales, unless a Beat has been sold exclusively through the Platform, in which case the terms of Section 3.3 shall apply.

---

## 2. PRODUCER OBLIGATIONS

**2.1 Original Content.** Producer represents and warrants that all Beats uploaded to the Platform are **original works** created by the Producer, and that Producer has full authority and legal right to distribute, license, and sell such Beats. Producer warrants that no Beat infringes upon the copyrights, trademarks, intellectual property rights, rights of publicity, or any other rights of any third party.

**2.2 Sample Clearance.** If any Beat contains samples from third-party recordings or compositions, Producer warrants that all necessary licenses, clearances, and permissions have been obtained prior to listing the Beat on the Platform. Producer shall provide proof of such clearances upon request by the Company.

**2.3 Accurate Information.** Producer agrees to provide accurate and complete information regarding each Beat, including but not limited to: title, tempo (BPM), key, genre/tags, and any applicable usage restrictions.

**2.4 Pricing.** Producer shall set their own prices for each Beat and each license tier (MP3 Lease, Trackout Lease, Exclusive Rights) offered through the Platform. Producer may update pricing at any time, subject to Section 2.5.

**2.5 Pending Transactions.** Price changes shall not affect any transactions that are in progress, pending, or already completed at the time of the price change.

**2.6 Platform Guidelines.** Producer agrees to comply with all Platform guidelines, community standards, and content policies as published on the Platform website and as may be amended from time to time with reasonable notice to the Producer.

---

## 3. BEAT OWNERSHIP AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**3.1 Retained Ownership.** Producer retains full ownership of all copyrights, intellectual property rights, and all other rights in and to each Beat, except to the extent that rights are transferred through an exclusive sale as described in Section 3.3.

**3.2 Non-Exclusive Licenses Sold.** When a non-exclusive license (MP3 Lease or Trackout Lease) is sold through the Platform, Producer retains ownership of the Beat and may continue to lease the Beat to additional Buyers.

**3.3 Exclusive Sales.** When an exclusive license is sold through the Platform:

- **(a)** The Beat shall be **immediately removed** from the Platform and shall no longer be available for purchase.
- **(b)** Producer shall not sell, license, or distribute the Beat to any new Buyers after the exclusive sale is completed.
- **(c)** All **previously sold non-exclusive licenses remain valid** and enforceable according to their original terms until they expire or are otherwise terminated.
- **(d)** Ownership and rights transfer shall be governed by the applicable Exclusive Rights License Agreement executed between Producer and Buyer.

**3.4 Producer's Publishing Rights.** Unless otherwise negotiated in writing between Producer and Buyer, Producer retains songwriting and publishing credit for the underlying musical composition of the Beat, even in the case of an exclusive sale.

---

## 4. REVENUE AND COMPENSATION

**4.1 Platform Commission.** The Company shall retain a commission of **[X]%** of the gross sale price for each Beat license sold through the Platform (the "Platform Commission"). The Producer shall receive the remaining **[100-X]%** of the gross sale price (the "Producer Share").

**4.2 Price Setting.** All prices displayed on the Platform are set by the Producer. The Platform Commission is calculated on the final sale price paid by the Buyer.

**4.3 Payment Processing.** All payments from Buyers are processed through the Platform's designated payment processor (currently **Stripe**). The Company utilizes **Stripe Connect** to facilitate direct payouts to Producers.

**4.4 Payout Schedule.** Producer payouts shall be made on a **[monthly / bi-weekly]** basis, subject to the following conditions:

- **(a)** Producer must have a valid and verified Stripe Connect account linked to the Platform.
- **(b)** A minimum balance of **$[MINIMUM_PAYOUT]** must be accrued before a payout is initiated, unless otherwise agreed.
- **(c)** Payouts are subject to standard payment processing hold periods as determined by the payment processor.

**4.5 Taxes.** Producer is solely responsible for reporting and paying all applicable federal, state, and local taxes on income earned through the Platform. The Company may issue IRS Form 1099-K or 1099-MISC as required by law. Producer agrees to provide accurate tax information (including W-9 or equivalent) upon request.

**4.6 Refunds and Chargebacks.** In the event of a refund or chargeback initiated by a Buyer, the Platform Commission and Producer Share for the affected transaction shall both be reversed. If a payout has already been made to Producer for a refunded or charged-back transaction, the reversed amount may be deducted from future payouts.

**4.7 Currency.** All transactions and payouts shall be denominated in United States Dollars (USD) unless otherwise specified.

## 5. CONTENT REMOVAL AND MODERATION

**5.1 Company's Right to Remove.** The Company reserves the right to remove, disable, or restrict access to any Beat or content on the Platform at its sole discretion, including but not limited to situations where:

- **(a)** The content is alleged or found to infringe on the intellectual property rights of a third party.
- **(b)** The content violates Platform guidelines, community standards, or applicable law.
- **(c)** The Company receives a valid complaint, takedown notice, or legal demand related to the content.
- **(d)** The content is deemed offensive, harmful, or inappropriate by the Company.

**5.2 Notice to Producer.** The Company shall make reasonable efforts to notify Producer of any content removal and the reason therefor, except where prohibited by law or where immediate removal is necessary to comply with legal obligations.

**5.3 Producer's Right to Remove.** Producer may request removal of any Beat from the Platform at any time by submitting a removal request through the Platform dashboard or by contacting the Company in writing. Removal shall be processed within a reasonable time, subject to the following:

- **(a)** Any pending or in-progress transactions for the Beat must be fulfilled before removal is completed.
- **(b)** Previously sold licenses for the removed Beat remain valid and enforceable according to their original terms.
- **(c)** Removal of a Beat does not affect any obligations under previously executed license agreements.

## 10. TERM AND TERMINATION

**10.1 Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in effect until terminated by either Party in accordance with this Section 10 (the "Term").

**10.2 Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement at any time, for any reason or no reason, by providing **thirty (30) days' written notice** to the other Party.

**10.3 Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

- **(a)** Materially breaches any provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within fifteen (15) days after receiving written notice thereof.
- **(b)** Becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or has a receiver appointed for a substantial portion of its assets.
- **(c)** Engages in fraud, criminal activity, or conduct that materially harms the other Party's reputation.

**10.4 Effects of Termination.** Upon termination of this Agreement:

- **(a)** All Beats shall be removed from the Platform within a reasonable time.
- **(b)** Any pending transactions at the time of termination shall be fulfilled, and Producer shall receive the corresponding Producer Share.
- **(c)** All previously sold licenses remain valid and enforceable according to their original terms.
- **(d)** Any outstanding payments owed to Producer shall be paid within thirty (30) days of the effective termination date.
- **(e)** Sections 3, 6, 7, 8, 9, and 11 shall survive termination.
