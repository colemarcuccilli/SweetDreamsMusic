## 1. BOOKING AND DEPOSITS

**1.1 Deposit Requirement.** A **non-refundable deposit of fifty percent (50%)** of the estimated session total is required to confirm a booking. The session is not confirmed until the deposit has been received and processed.

**1.2 Deposit Payment.** Deposits may be paid through the Sweet Dreams Music website, dashboard, or via direct payment link. All payments are processed through the Platform's designated payment processor (currently Stripe).

**1.3 Remaining Balance.** The remaining balance is due **at the time of the session** or will be charged to the credit/debit card on file. If the session runs longer than the originally booked duration, the additional time will be charged at the applicable hourly rate.

**1.4 Card on File.** By booking a session, Client authorizes Sweet Dreams Music to keep a payment method on file and to charge the remaining balance and any applicable surcharges or fees as described in this Agreement.

---

## 2. PRICING AND SURCHARGES

**2.1 Standard Rate.** The standard hourly rate for recording sessions is as listed on the Sweet Dreams Music website or as quoted at the time of booking.

**2.2 Same-Day Booking Surcharge.** Sessions booked on the **same day** as the session date are subject to a surcharge of **$10.00 per hour** in addition to the standard hourly rate.

**2.3 Late Night Surcharges.** Sessions that extend into or are booked during late night/early morning hours are subject to the following surcharges **in addition to** the standard hourly rate:

| Time Window | Surcharge |
|-------------|-----------|
| **10:00 PM — 2:00 AM** | **+$10.00 per hour** |
| **2:00 AM — 9:00 AM** | **+$30.00 per hour** |

**2.4 Surcharge Calculation.** Surcharges are calculated on a per-hour basis for the portion of the session that falls within the applicable time window. If a session spans multiple surcharge windows, each portion is charged at the corresponding surcharge rate.

**2.5 Additional Services.** Any additional services requested during the session (e.g., additional mixing, mastering, special equipment rental) will be quoted separately and charged in addition to the session rate.

---

## 3. CANCELLATION AND RESCHEDULING

**3.1 Cancellation Policy.** Deposits are **non-refundable**. If Client cancels a session for any reason, the deposit is forfeited.

**3.2 Rescheduling.** Client may reschedule a session **at no additional charge** provided that the rescheduling request is made at least **twenty-four (24) hours** before the originally scheduled session start time.

**3.3 Late Rescheduling.** Rescheduling requests made **less than twenty-four (24) hours** before the session start time will be treated as a cancellation. The deposit will be forfeited, and a new deposit will be required to book a new session.

**3.4 No-Shows.** If Client fails to arrive for a scheduled session without prior notice ("No-Show"), the deposit is forfeited and the full session amount may be charged to the card on file.

**3.5 Studio-Initiated Cancellation.** In the event that Sweet Dreams Music must cancel or reschedule a session due to equipment failure, staffing issues, emergencies, or other circumstances beyond reasonable control, Client will be offered the choice of:

- **(a)** Rescheduling to the next available time at no additional cost; or
- **(b)** Receiving a full refund of the deposit.

## 4. SESSION POLICIES

**4.1 Arrival Time.** Client is expected to arrive at or before the scheduled session start time. **Session time begins at the booked start time regardless of when the Client arrives.** Late arrivals will not result in an extension of the session unless additional time is available and agreed upon, subject to the applicable hourly rate.

**4.2 Session Extensions.** If Client wishes to extend a session beyond the originally booked duration, extensions are subject to studio and engineer availability. Extended time will be charged at the applicable hourly rate plus any applicable surcharges.

**4.3 Guest Policy.** Client may bring a reasonable number of guests to the session (generally no more than **three (3) additional persons** unless pre-approved). All guests must comply with studio rules. Sweet Dreams Music reserves the right to limit the number of guests at its discretion for safety, space, or quality-of-session reasons.

**4.4 Minors.** Clients under the age of 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian, who must co-sign this Agreement and accept responsibility for the minor's conduct and financial obligations.

## 5. STUDIO RULES AND CONDUCT

**5.1 Prohibited Activities.** The following activities are **strictly prohibited** on Sweet Dreams Music premises:

- **(a) No smoking, vaping, or use of tobacco products** inside the studio or recording areas.
- **(b) No weapons** of any kind (firearms, knives, or other weapons) on the premises.
- **(c) No illegal drugs or controlled substances** on the premises.
- **(d) No unauthorized guests** — all persons present must be known to and approved by the Client and studio staff.
- **(e) No food or open beverages** near studio equipment (designated eating areas will be provided if available).
- **(f) No tampering with, adjusting, or moving studio equipment** without the engineer's permission.
- **(g) No recording of the studio session on personal devices** (audio or video) without prior consent from all parties present.

**5.2 Respect for Equipment.** Client and all guests are responsible for treating studio equipment and facilities with care. Client shall be financially responsible for any damage to studio equipment, furniture, or facilities caused by Client or Client's guests during the session.

**5.3 Conduct.** Sweet Dreams Music reserves the right to **immediately terminate any session** without refund if Client or any guest engages in behavior that is:

- **(a)** Threatening, violent, or abusive toward studio staff or other clients.
- **(b)** Destructive to studio property or equipment.
- **(c)** In violation of any studio rule listed in this Section.
- **(d)** Otherwise disruptive to the operation of the studio.

**5.4 Alcohol.** Moderate, responsible consumption of alcohol by persons of legal drinking age (21+) is permitted at the studio's discretion, provided it does not interfere with the session or violate any other studio rule.
