# Sweet Dreams Music — Blog & Education Content Plan

## Strategy
Turn Sweet Dreams Music into a music education authority. Each blog post covers a topic from real university music programs (Berklee, NYU, Belmont, USC). This builds SEO, establishes expertise, and gives artists real value — the same knowledge a $200K degree teaches, free on our platform.

**Target**: 150+ blog posts covering the equivalent of a Bachelor's + Master's in Music Business, Production, and Artist Development.

**Audience**: Independent artists, aspiring producers, engineers, and music entrepreneurs in Fort Wayne and beyond.

**SEO Goal**: Rank for "music theory basics", "how to copyright a song", "recording studio tips", "beat licensing explained", etc.

## Publishing Schedule

**Phase 1 (Month 1-3)**: 3 posts/week = 36 posts
- Focus on SEO foundation: Music Theory, Production basics, Business essentials
- Targets highest-search-volume keywords first

**Phase 2 (Month 4-6)**: 2 posts/week = 24 posts
- Marketing, Artist Development, advanced production
- Internal linking to existing posts

**Phase 3 (Month 7-12)**: 1-2 posts/week = 40-80 posts
- History, instruments, niche topics
- Complete the full curriculum

**Total**: 140 posts = equivalent of a 6-year music education

## Blog Features to Build

1. **Category pages** with filterable post lists
2. **Reading progress bar** on articles
3. **"Related posts" recommendations** at bottom
4. **Email capture** ("Get the next lesson free")
5. **Series/course grouping** ("Music Theory 101" as a linked series)

7. **Table of contents** for long posts
8. **Audio examples** embedded where applicable (use beats from the store)
